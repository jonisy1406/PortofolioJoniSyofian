Context

This project supported oceanographic research on Cirebon coastal waters. The work contributed to a scientific reference published by ITB Press, titled Pengamatan Perairan Cirebon.

Contribution

  • Supported field-based coastal water data collection for oceanographic observation.
  • Processed oceanographic datasets using MATLAB for analysis and visualization.
  • Analyzed nutrient and heavy metal distribution patterns in Cirebon waters.
  • Generated visual outputs to help communicate spatial and environmental insights.
  • Contributed to published scientific material by ITB Press as one of the listed authors.

Research Focus

The publication discusses observations and studies around Cirebon waters and coastal areas, including research related to nutrient distribution and pollutants such as heavy metals.
